SAYING NO
  • "Let me think about it and get back to you" is a complete answer.
  • One reason maximum. Zero is allowed.
  • Explaining more is not persuading more. It is negotiating against yourself.
  • Say it with the shake. The shake is not visible from outside.
  • Their anger is not evidence you are wrong. It is evidence they wanted a yes.
  • The silence after your no is their turn. Do not fill it.

FACT vs FEELING
"Work was busy""I felt useless at work today."
"The trip was fine""I felt close to you on that trip."
"Traffic was bad""I felt cornered all day."
"The meeting ran long""I felt dismissed in that meeting."
"I am tired""I felt defeated today."
If the sentence works without the word "I felt," it is a fact. Facts do not build closeness.
